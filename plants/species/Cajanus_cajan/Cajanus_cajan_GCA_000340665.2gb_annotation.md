**Annotation**
----------

The annotation presented is derived from genes imported from the community GFF3 with the assembly accession [GCA\_000340665.2](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/GCA_000340665.2).

Genomic annotation was deposited along with initial assembly submission by ["BGI"](https://english.big.cas.cn/).

Small RNA features, protein features, BLAST hits and cross-references have been computed by [Ensembl Plants](https://plants.ensembl.org/info/genome/annotation/index.html).
