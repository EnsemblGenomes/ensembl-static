**About *Cynara cardunculus***
-------------------------
The artichoke (*Cynara cardunculus var. scolymus*), also known by the other names: French artichoke, globe artichoke, and green artichoke in the United States, is a variety of a species of thistle cultivated as food. It belongs to the Compositae (a.k.a. Asteraceae), one of the most successful Angiosperm families. *Cynara cardunculus* is a diploid species (2n=2x=34) with a medium-sized genome.
