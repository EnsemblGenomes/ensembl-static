**Annotation**
----------

The annotation presented is derived from genes imported from the community GFF3 with the assembly accession [GCA_001531365.2](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/view/GCA_001531365.2)

Genomic annotation was deposited along with initial assembly submission by Compositae Genome Project.

Small RNA features, protein features, BLAST hits and cross-references have been computed by [Ensembl Plants](https://plants.ensembl.org/info/genome/annotation/index.html).
