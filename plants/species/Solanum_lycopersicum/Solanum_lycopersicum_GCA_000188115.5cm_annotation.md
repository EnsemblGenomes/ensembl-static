**Annotation**
----------

The annotation presented is derived from genes imported from the community GFF3 with the assembly accession [GCA\_000188115.5](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/GCA_000188115.5).

Genomic annotation was deposited along with initial assembly submission by ["Solanaceae Genomics Project"](https://solgenomics.net/organism/Solanum_lycopersicum/genome).

Small RNA features, protein features, BLAST hits and cross-references have been computed by [Ensembl Plants](https://plants.ensembl.org/info/genome/annotation/index.html).
