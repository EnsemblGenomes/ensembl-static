## Pangenomes

Ensembl Plants hosts the following four pangenomes:

- The barley pangenome comprises 77 wild and domesticated [barley accessions](https://plants.ensembl.org/Hordeum_vulgare/Info/Cultivars?db=core)
- The oat pangenome that includes 33 wild and domesticated [oat lines](https://plants.ensembl.org/species.html)
- The rice pangenome consists of 26 *Oryza* genomes including [16 *Oryza sativa* cultivars](https://plants.ensembl.org/Oryza_sativa/Info/Cultivars?db=core)
- The wheat pangenome consists of 24 *Triticum* genomes including [19 *Triticum aestivum* cultivars](https://plants.ensembl.org/Triticum_aestivum/Info/Cultivars?db=core)
