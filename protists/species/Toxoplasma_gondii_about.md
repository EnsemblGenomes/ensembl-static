**About *Toxoplasma gondii***
-------------------------
*Toxoplasma gondii* is a species of parasitic alveolate that causes 
toxoplasmosis. Found worldwide, T. gondii is capable of infecting 
virtually all warm-blooded animals, but members of the cat family 
(felidae) are the only known definitive hosts in which the parasite 
may undergo sexual reproduction.
