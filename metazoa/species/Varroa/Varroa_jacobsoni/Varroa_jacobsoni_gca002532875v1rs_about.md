**About *Varroa jacobsoni***
-------------------------
*Varroa jacobsoni* is a species of mite that parasitises Apis cerana. 
The more damaging Varroa destructor was previously included under the 
name V. jacobsoni, but the two species can be separated on the basis 
of the DNA (*Varroa jacobsoni*) sequence of the cytochrome oxidase I 
gene in the mitochondrial DNA.
