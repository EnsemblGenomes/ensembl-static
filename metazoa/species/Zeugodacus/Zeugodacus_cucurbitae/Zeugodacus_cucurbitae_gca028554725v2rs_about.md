**About *Zeugodacus cucurbitae***
-------------------------
Bactrocera cucurbitae, the melon fly (*Zeugodacus cucurbitae*), is a 
fruit fly of the family Tephritidae. It is a serious agricultural 
pest, particularly in Hawaii.
