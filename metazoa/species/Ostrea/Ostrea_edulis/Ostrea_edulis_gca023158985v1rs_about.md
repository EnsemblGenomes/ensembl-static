**About *Ostrea edulis***
-------------------------
*Ostrea edulis*, commonly known as the European flat oyster, is a 
species of oyster native to Europe. In Britain and Ireland, regional 
names include Colchester native oyster, mud oyster, or edible oyster. 
In France, Ostrea edulis are known as huîtres plates except for those 
that come from the Belon River estuary in Brittany, France, which are 
known as Belons.
