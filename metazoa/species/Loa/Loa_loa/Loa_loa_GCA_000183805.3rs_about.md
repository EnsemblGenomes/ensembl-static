**About *Loa loa***
-------------------------
*Loa loa* is a filarial (arthropod-borne) nematode (roundworm) that 
causes Loa loa filariasis. Loa loa actually means "worm worm", but is 
commonly known as the "eye worm", as it localizes to the conjunctiva 
of the eye. Loa loa is commonly found in Africa. It mainly inhabits 
rain forests in West Africa and has native origins in Ethiopia. The 
disease caused by Loa loa is called loiasis and is one of the 
neglected tropical diseases.
